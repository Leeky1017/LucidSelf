## PART 1: House Philosophy

### 1. Locality-Centered vs Globe-Centered (地方中心vs地球中心)

**L1**: Archaic astrology = **locality-centered** (tribal, bounded by horizon, local sky). Modern astrology = **globe-centered** (geocentric, rational horizon through Earth center). Shift from tribe to individual, soil-bound to mobile consciousness. 古占星=**地方中心**(部落、地平线界定、本地天空)。现代占星=**地球中心**(地心、理性地平线穿地心)。从部落到个人、从土地束缚到流动意识转变。

**L2**: Cosmological shift. Function: Tribal → Individual consciousness, local → global perspective. East: 地方中心↔本地坐标/地方时，地球中心↔全球视野/地心坐标。

---

### 2. Eight Watches (Archaic Vitalistic) (八更/古代生命节奏)

**L1**: Archaic houses = **8 watches** (3-hour periods), based on Sun's daily path. **Clockwise** sequence (sunrise→culmination→sunset→midnight). Vitalistic, agricultural rhythms, Sun-dominant. Simple divisions of solar day. 古代宫位=**八更**(3小时周期)，基于太阳日行。**顺时针**序列(日出→正午→日落→午夜)。生命节奏、农业韵律、太阳主导。太阳日简单分割。

**L2**: Vitalistic rhythm. Function: Solar life-force pattern, agricultural society. East: 八更↔十二时辰/八刻/更漏（生命节奏）。

---

### 3. Twelve Houses (Modern Psychomental) (十二宫/现代心智)

**L1**: Modern houses = **12 sectors** (30° each), **counterclockwise** sequence (Earth rotation perspective). Psychomental rhythm opposes vitalistic. Individual consciousness unfolds Asc→MC→Desc→IC. Person-centered, not Sun-centered. 现代宫位=**12扇区**(各30°)，**逆时针**序列(地球自转视角)。心智节奏对立生命节奏。个人意识展开上升→天顶→下降→天底。以人为中心非太阳中心。

**L2**: Psychomental development. Function: Individual consciousness unfoldment vs vitalistic rhythm. East: 十二宫↔命宫系统/逆时针展开（心智成长）。

---

### 4. Four Angles (Cardinal Cross) (四角/基本十字)

**L1**: **Asc/Desc** (horizon) + **MC/IC** (meridian) = **cross of matter**, cardinal points. Asc = birth/dawn/self. MC = culmination/noon/social achievement. Desc = relationship/sunset/other. IC = roots/midnight/foundation. Most powerful chart points. **上升/下降**(地平线) + **天顶/天底**(子午线) = **物质十字**、基本点。上升=出生/黎明/自我。天顶=顶点/正午/社会成就。下降=关系/日落/他人。天底=根基/午夜/基础。最强命盘点。

**L2**: Cardinal cross. Function: Four life orientation points (self/achievement/other/roots). East: 四角↔四正宫/命身福三角/关键宫位。

---

### 5. Zodiac (Celestial) vs Houses (Terrestrial) (星座天vs宫位地)

**L1**: **Zodiac = celestial/orbital** (Earth's yearly path around Sun, spatial-cosmic). **Houses = terrestrial/rotational** (Earth's daily spin, durational-local). Two frames of reference: space (zodiac) vs time (houses). Sky-Earth polarity. **星座=天上/轨道**(地球年行绕日、空间-宇宙)。**宫位=地上/自转**(地球日旋、时间-本地)。两个参照系：空间(星座) vs 时间(宫位)。天地极性。

**L2**: Dual framework. Function: Celestial space (zodiac) vs terrestrial time (houses). East: 天地两仪↔星座(天)/宫位(地)。

---

### 6. Sidereal vs Tropical Zodiac Debate (恒星vs回归星座辩论)

**L1**: **Sidereal** = constellations (pantheistic, space hierarchies). **Tropical** = Earth's orbit (monotheistic, Earth-Sun relationship). Rudhyar advocates tropical for modern consciousness. Precession creates 23° discrepancy. Different cosmologies. **恒星**=星群(泛神论、空间层级)。**回归**=地球轨道(一神论、地日关系)。Rudhyar主张回归适合现代意识。岁差造成23°差异。不同宇宙观。

**L2**: Cosmological choice. Function: Space-centered (sidereal) vs relationship-centered (tropical). East: 恒星vs回归↔天星vs节气(类似争论)。

---

### 7. Monotheistic vs Pantheistic Cosmology (一神vs泛神宇宙观)

**L1**: **Monotheistic** (tropical): Sun = one God, Earth-Sun dialogue, orbit = 12 soul avenues. **Pantheistic** (sidereal): 12 constellations = creative hierarchies, Sun = lens/window for galactic energies. Fundamental philosophical difference. **一神论**(回归)：太阳=唯一神、地日对话、轨道=12灵魂道路。**泛神论**(恒星)：12星群=创造层级、太阳=银河能量透镜/窗口。根本哲学差异。

**L2**: Theological foundation. Function: One God (Sun) vs cosmic hierarchies (constellations). East: 一神vs泛神↔天帝vs众神/道统。

## PART 2: The Twelve Houses (Humanistic Interpretation)

### 8. House 1 - Self/Identity (第一宫-自我/身份)

**L1**: **Ascendant/Rising**, point of birth, dawn of consciousness. Self-image, personality expression, how others see you first. **Angular** (action), cardinal fire analog (Aries). Personal initiative, self-assertion. Beginning of life journey. **上升点**，出生点、意识黎明。自我形象、人格表达、他人初见你方式。**始宫**(行动)，类似基本火相(白羊)。个人主动、自我主张。生命旅程开始。

**L2**: Self-manifestation. Function: Identity, personality, first impression. East: 第一宫↔命宫/身宫/自我呈现。

---

### 9. House 2 - Resources/Values (第二宫-资源/价值)

**L1**: Personal resources, possessions, self-worth, values. What you own materially & psychologically. **Succedent** (consolidation), fixed earth analog (Taurus). Building security, establishing worth. Substance accumulated. 个人资源、财产、自我价值、价值观。你拥有什么(物质&心理)。**续宫**(巩固)，类似固定土相(金牛)。建立安全、确立价值。积累实质。

**L2**: Resource building. Function: Material/psychological assets, self-valuation. East: 第二宫↔财帛宫/价值积累。

---

### 10. House 3 - Communication/Learning (第三宫-沟通/学习)

**L1**: Mind, communication, siblings, short trips, early education. Immediate environment exploration. **Cadent** (distribution), mutable air analog (Gemini). Information exchange, mental connections. Local relationships. 心智、沟通、手足、短途、早期教育。即时环境探索。**果宫**(分配)，类似变动风相(双子)。信息交换、心智连接。本地关系。

**L2**: Mental exchange. Function: Communication, learning, local environment. East: 第三宫↔兄弟宫/沟通学习。

---

### 11. House 4 - Home/Roots (第四宫-家庭/根基)

**L1**: **IC/Nadir**, midnight, deepest foundation. Home, family, ancestry, emotional security. Private self, roots. **Angular** (action), cardinal water analog (Cancer). Inner life, psychological foundation. End of matter (vs beginning at Asc). **天底**，午夜、最深基础。家、家族、祖先、情感安全。私密自我、根源。**始宫**(行动)，类似基本水相(巨蟹)。内在生活、心理基础。事物终结(vs上升开始)。

**L2**: Emotional foundation. Function: Home, family, roots, inner security. East: 第四宫↔田宅宫/家庭根基。

---

### 12. House 5 - Creativity/Expression (第五宫-创造/表达)

**L1**: Self-expression, creativity, children, romance, joy. Radiating self outward. **Succedent** (consolidation), fixed fire analog (Leo). Creative power, love affairs, speculation. Ego expression. 自我表达、创造、子女、恋爱、欢乐。向外辐射自我。**续宫**(巩固)，类似固定火相(狮子)。创造力、恋情、投机。自我表达。

**L2**: Creative radiance. Function: Self-expression, creativity, joy, romance. East: 第五宫↔子女宫/创造表达。

---

### 13. House 6 - Service/Work (第六宫-服务/工作)

**L1**: Daily work, health, service, duty. Self-improvement, technique mastery. **Cadent** (distribution), mutable earth analog (Virgo). Refining self through work, health habits. Crisis of self-adjustment. 日常工作、健康、服务、责任。自我改善、技术掌握。**果宫**(分配)，类似变动土相(处女)。通过工作精炼自我、健康习惯。自我调整危机。

**L2**: Self-refinement. Function: Work, health, service, technique. East: 第六宫↔奴仆宫/工作健康。

---

### 14. House 7 - Relationship/Partnership (第七宫-关系/伙伴)

**L1**: **Descendant**, sunset, others. Marriage, partnerships, open enemies. Projected self, what we seek in others. **Angular** (action), cardinal air analog (Libra). One-to-one relationships, contracts. Balancing self with other. **下降点**，日落、他人。婚姻、伙伴、公开敌人。投射自我、我们在他人中寻求什么。**始宫**(行动)，类似基本风相(天秤)。一对一关系、契约。平衡自我与他人。

**L2**: Partnership polarity. Function: Marriage, partnerships, relating to others. East: 第七宫↔夫妻宫/伙伴关系。

---

### 15. House 8 - Transformation/Shared Resources (第八宫-转化/共享资源)

**L1**: Death/rebirth, transformation, shared resources, sex, occult. Partner's values, deep bonding. **Succedent** (consolidation), fixed water analog (Scorpio). Psychological depth, crisis transformation. Merging resources. 死亡/重生、转化、共享资源、性、神秘。伙伴价值、深度连结。**续宫**(巩固)，类似固定水相(天蝎)。心理深度、危机转化。融合资源。

**L2**: Deep transformation. Function: Death/rebirth, shared resources, intimacy. East: 第八宫↔疾厄宫/转化死生。

---

### 16. House 9 - Philosophy/Expansion (第九宫-哲学/扩展)

**L1**: Higher mind, philosophy, religion, long journeys, higher education. Seeking meaning beyond personal. **Cadent** (distribution), mutable fire analog (Sagittarius). Wisdom quest, cultural expansion. Abstract thinking. 高等心智、哲学、宗教、远行、高等教育。寻求超越个人意义。**果宫**(分配)，类似变动火相(射手)。智慧追求、文化扩展。抽象思维。

**L2**: Meaning quest. Function: Philosophy, religion, higher learning, expansion. East: 第九宫↔迁移宫/哲学扩展。

---

### 17. House 10 - Career/Public Life (第十宫-事业/公众生活)

**L1**: **MC/Midheaven**, noon, culmination. Career, public reputation, authority, achievements. Social self, vocation. **Angular** (action), cardinal earth analog (Capricorn). Worldly success, responsibility. Peak of manifestation. **天顶**，正午、顶点。事业、公众声誉、权威、成就。社会自我、天职。**始宫**(行动)，类似基本土相(摩羯)。世俗成功、责任。显化顶峰。

**L2**: Social achievement. Function: Career, reputation, public role, authority. East: 第十宫↔官禄宫/事业成就。

---

### 18. House 11 - Community/Ideals (第十一宫-社群/理想)

**L1**: Friends, groups, social causes, hopes/wishes, future vision. Collective participation. **Succedent** (consolidation), fixed air analog (Aquarius). Social idealism, group consciousness. Reforming society. 朋友、团体、社会理想、希望/愿望、未来愿景。集体参与。**续宫**(巩固)，类似固定风相(水瓶)。社会理想主义、群体意识。改革社会。

**L2**: Social idealism. Function: Friends, groups, ideals, future hopes. East: 第十一宫↔福德宫/社群理想。

---

### 19. House 12 - Transcendence/Dissolution (第十二宫-超越/消解)

**L1**: Unconscious, hidden enemies, self-undoing, transcendence, karma. Retreat, hospitals, prisons. **Cadent** (distribution), mutable water analog (Pisces). Dissolution of ego, mystical union. Ending before new beginning (→H1). 无意识、隐藏敌人、自我消解、超越、业力。隐退、医院、监狱。**果宫**(分配)，类似变动水相(双鱼)。自我消解、神秘合一。结束于新开始前(→第1宫)。

**L2**: Ego dissolution. Function: Unconscious, transcendence, karma, endings. East: 第十二宫↔相貌宫/超越消解。

### 20. Angular-Succedent-Cadent Classification (始-续-果分类)
