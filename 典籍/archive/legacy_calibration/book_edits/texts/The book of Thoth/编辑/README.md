## Historical Context

**Aleister Crowley (1875-1947)**:
- British occultist, poet, ceremonial magician
- Member of Hermetic Order of the Golden Dawn
- Founded Thelema philosophy ("Do what thou wilt shall be the whole of the Law")
- Collaborated with artist Lady Frieda Harris to create Thoth deck (1938-1943)

**The Book of Thoth**:
- Comprehensive guide to Crowley's tarot deck
- Deep Qabalistic correlations (Tree of Life, Sepher Yetzirah)
- Astrological, alchemical, mythological layers
- Considered advanced text requiring occult background
