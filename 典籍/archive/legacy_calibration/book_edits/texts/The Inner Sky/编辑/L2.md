## I. Core Terminology

### A. Chart Structure

**natal_chart**: Snapshot of solar system at exact birth moment/location. Contains planets in signs and houses. Requires exact time for houses.

**planet**: Psychological function (WHAT). 10 bodies: Sun→Pluto. Active "verbs" of astrology.

**sign**: Expression mode (HOW). 12 signs. Passive "adjectives." Has element (Fire/Earth/Air/Water) and mode (Cardinal/Fixed/Mutable).

**house**: Life arena (WHERE). 12 departments. Time-sensitive. 1st cusp = Ascendant.

**Grammar**: Planet+Sign+House = complete statement. E.g., "Mars in Aries in 7th" = assertiveness boldly in relationships.

---

### B. Modifiers

**retrograde**: Apparent backward motion. Energy turns inward. Creates depth, independence, shyness. Sun/Moon never retrograde.

**stationary**: About to turn retrograde/direct. Volume amplified. Stubborn, dominant.

**ascendant**: Rising sign. Eastern horizon degree. Surface self, mask, first impression. NOT core identity (Sun) or feelings (Moon).

## II. Planet Semantics

### Sun
- **Function**: Ego formation, will, identity
- **Feed**: Experiences matching its sign
- **Starve**: Blahs, ritual, fragmentation
- **Shadow**: Vanity, tyranny, selfishness
- **Note**: Center that organizes all other functions

### Moon
- **Function**: Feeling, emotional response, soul
- **Nature**: Mutable, responsive, irrational
- **Feed**: Experiences fulfilling its sign needs
- **Starve**: Moodiness, depression, emptiness
- **Shadow**: Indulgence, laziness, overwhelm
- **Note**: Gives life meaning and color

### Mercury
- **Function**: Thinking, communication, perception
- **Speed**: Fastest (30 mi/sec) = quickest mental processes
- **Risk**: Bound to Sun (max 28°), can rationalize ego's biases
- **Retrograde**: Innovative thinking, possible inarticulation
- **Shadow**: Nervousness, rationalization, chatter
- **Note**: Information processor, not wisdom

### Venus
- **Function**: Harmony, relationships, beauty
- **Domain**: Love, aesthetics, peace ("heaven's Valium")
- **Proximity**: Closest to Earth, max 48° from Sun
- **Retrograde**: Social shyness, creative freedom
- **Shadow**: Vanity, manipulation, laziness
- **Note**: Attraction and aesthetic response

### Mars
- **Function**: Will, courage, assertiveness
- **Independence**: First planet beyond Earth (visual freedom)
- **Feed**: Stress, challenge, adventure
- **Starve**: Irritability, pointless conflict
- **Retrograde**: Passive exterior, endurance, internalized anger
- **Shadow**: Rage, cruelty, misdirected aggression
- **Imperative**: "Face the bears"

## IV. Semantic Framework

### Function/Dysfunction Pattern
- **Function**: Healthy planetary expression
- **Dysfunction**: Distorted/excessive expression (usually from starvation)

### Sign Growth Model
- **Endpoint**: Evolutionary goal (direction not destination)
- **Strategy**: Methods to reach endpoint
- **Resources**: Natural gifts supporting strategy
- **Shadow**: Failure mode when growth stops

### House vs Sign
- **Signs**: Objective (same for everyone at same time)
- **Houses**: Subjective (unique to exact time/place)
- **Houses**: WHERE behavior focuses, not character traits

### Polarities
Opposite signs balance each other (not conflict):
- Aries-Libra: Self vs Other
- Taurus-Scorpio: Having vs Letting Go
- Gemini-Sagittarius: Info vs Meaning

## IV. Expanded Semantic Framework

### Element Patterns (Complete)
- **Fire** (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius): Action, enthusiasm, direct expression, will
- **Earth** (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn): Manifestation, practicality, tangible results, discipline
- **Water** (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces): Feeling, intuition, emotional connection, empathy
- **Air** (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius): Thought, communication, detachment, ideas

### Mode Patterns (Complete)
- **Cardinal** (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn): Initiation, leadership, starting action
- **Fixed** (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius): Stability, persistence, maintaining direction
- **Mutable** (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces): Adaptation, flexibility, change

### Outer vs Inner Planets
- **Personal Planets** (Sun→Mars): Direct personality expression, fast-moving
- **Social Planets** (Jupiter, Saturn): Interface with society, medium-speed
- **Outer Planets** (Uranus→Pluto, not yet covered): Generational, slow-moving

### Planet-Planet Dynamics
- **Jupiter-Saturn Cycle**: Expansion vs consolidation, faith vs realism
- **Sun-Moon**: Identity vs emotion, ego vs soul
- **Mercury-Venus**: Bound to Sun (max 28°/48°), serve ego function

### Sign Growth Stages
All signs follow: **Endpoint → Strategy → Resources → Shadow (when stuck)**

Shadow emerges when:
- Strategy is avoided (Aries avoids standing alone → chronic fear)
- Resources are misused (Leo's confidence → arrogance)
- Endpoint is perverted (Virgo's excellence → paralytic perfectionism)

## VIII. Aspect Semantics (P2 Expansion)

### Major Aspects

| Aspect | Degrees | Nature | Function |
|--------|---------|--------|----------|
| **Conjunction** | 0° | Fusion | Planets merge energies; intensification |
| **Opposition** | 180° | Tension/Awareness | Polarized forces; external projection; relationship axis |
| **Trine** | 120° | Ease/Flow | Same element; natural harmony; talent (can be lazy) |
| **Square** | 90° | Friction/Growth | Different modes clash; dynamic tension; builds strength |
| **Sextile** | 60° | Opportunity | Compatible elements; requires effort to activate |

### Aspect Interpretation Framework

**Forrest approach** = Focus on **integration**, not prediction.

| Aspect | Challenge | Integration |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Conjunction | Over-identification; blind spot | Conscious integration of both functions |
| Opposition | Projection; either/or thinking | Both/and awareness; owning projected content |
| Square | Chronic friction; inner conflict | Creative tension → growth engine |
| Trine | Complacency; taking talent for granted | Conscious development of natural gifts |
| Sextile | Missed opportunities | Proactive engagement with compatible energies |

### Orbs and Strength

| Aspect | Tight Orb | Wide Orb | Effect of Tightness |
|--------|-----------|----------|---------------------|
| Conjunction | 0-3° | 3-8° | Exact = most intense fusion |
| Opposition | 0-3° | 3-8° | Exact = strongest polarity |
| Square | 0-3° | 3-6° | Exact = most friction |
| Trine | 0-3° | 3-6° | Exact = most ease |
| Sextile | 0-2° | 2-4° | Exact = clearest opportunity |

**Applying vs Separating**: Applying = building intensity; Separating = waning influence.

### Forrest's Integration Method
