## Toach's Christian Framework

**Core Principles**:
1. **Dreams as Divine Communication**: God speaks through dreams/visions (biblical tradition)
2. **Types & Shadows**: Biblical symbolic language (since Adam, Jesus spoke in parables)
3. **Spirit, Word, Real Life Balance**: Holy Spirit + Scripture + everyday context
4. **Everything Symbolic**: Dreams use familiar objects as spiritual symbols
5. **Everything About You**: Personal revelation for dreamer's spiritual journey

**Key Distinctions from Jung**:
- **Source**: Divine communication (not just psyche)
- **Purpose**: Spiritual guidance, destiny, warfare (not just integration)
- **Interpretation**: Biblical/spiritual meanings (not just psychological)
- **Goal**: Closer to God, fulfill calling (not just wholeness)

---

## Dream Types (Toach Classification)

**Three Main Types**:
1. **Purging Dream**: Processing/releasing emotional/spiritual baggage
2. **Garbage Dream**: Meaningless, result of daily stress (ignore)
3. **Internal Dream**: Significant spiritual message (focus interpretation here)

**Internal Dream Subtypes**:
- **Personal Growth**: Character development, sin patterns
- **Spiritual Warfare**: Demonic attack, intercession needs
- **Destiny/Calling**: Life purpose, ministry direction
- **Prophetic**: Future events, community guidance

## 4-Step Interpretation Method

**Toach's Framework**:
1. **Discern the Spirit**: From God, enemy, or flesh? (spiritual source)
2. **Everything is Symbolic**: Don't take literally, find spiritual meaning
3. **Everything is About You**: Personal revelation (not predicting others)
4. **Identify the Message**: What is God saying about your spiritual journey?

**Comparison to Other Methods**:
- **vs Freud**: No free association, direct spiritual meaning
- **vs Jung**: Not character aspects but spiritual realities
- **Unique**: Spiritual warfare discernment, biblical context priority
