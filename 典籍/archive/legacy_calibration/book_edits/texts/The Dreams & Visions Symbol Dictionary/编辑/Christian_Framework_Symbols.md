## PART 1: Christian Spiritual Framework

### 1. Divine Communication (神圣沟通)

**L1**: Core Toach thesis - **dreams = God speaking**. Biblical tradition since Adam, Jesus spoke in parables, God continues speaking through symbols. Dreams reveal mysteries of Kingdom (Mark 4:11). Not psychological integration but **divine revelation**. 核心论点——**梦=神说话**。自亚当圣经传统，耶稣说比喻，神继续通过象征说话。梦揭示天国奥秘(马可4:11)。非心理整合而是**神圣启示**。

**Key Term Analysis**:
| Term | Definition | Significance |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Divine Communication | Dreams = God speaking | Core thesis |
| Divine Revelation | Not psychology | Spiritual source |
| Kingdom Mysteries | Mark 4:11 | Biblical basis |

**Textual Criticism (Bilingual)**:
- **English**: Divine communication: dreams = God speaking. Biblical tradition. Not psychological but divine revelation.
- **中文**: 神圣沟通:梦=神说话。圣经传统。非心理而是神圣启示。

**L2**: Divine source. Function: God communicates destiny, guidance, warnings through dreams. East: 天启/神谕/天意（类似梦林玄解天命观）。

### 2. Types & Shadows (类型与阴影)

**L1**: Biblical symbolic language - God speaks in **types (预表)** and **shadows (影子)** since creation. Physical things represent spiritual realities. Jesus never spoke without parables. Familiar everyday objects = spiritual symbols. Not literal but typological interpretation. 圣经象征语言——神从创世说**预表**和**影子**。物质事物代表灵性实在。耶稣总说比喻。日常熟悉物品=灵性象征。非字面而是预表诠释。

**Key Term Analysis**:
| Term | Definition | Significance |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Types (预表) | Physical represents spiritual | Biblical method |
| Shadows (影子) | Foreshadowing reality | Typology |
| Typological | Not literal interpretation | Symbolic reading |

**Textual Criticism (Bilingual)**:
- **English**: Types & Shadows: biblical symbolic language. Physical = spiritual. Typological not literal.
- **中文**: 预表与影子:圣经象征语言。物质=灵性。预表诠释非字面。

**L2**: Biblical symbolism. Function: Physical symbols represent spiritual truths (typology). East: 象数理占/物象喻道（类似易经取象比类）。

---

### 3. Spirit-Word-Life Balance (灵-道-生命平衡)

**L1**: Toach's three-fold method - perfect interpretation requires **Holy Spirit** (brings Word to life) + **Scripture** (standard) + **Real Life** (familiar context). Balance all three, avoid extremes. Not Spirit alone (subjectivism) or Word alone (legalism) or Life alone (naturalism). **圣灵**(使道活现) + **圣经**(标准) + **现实生活**(熟悉语境)。平衡三者避免极端。非仅灵(主观主义)或仅道(律法主义)或仅生命(自然主义)。

**Key Term Analysis**:
| Term | Definition | Significance |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Spirit-Word-Life | Three-fold balance | Interpretation method |
| Holy Spirit | Brings Word to life | Revelation |
| Scripture | Standard/measure | Authority |

**Textual Criticism (Bilingual)**:
- **English**: Spirit-Word-Life balance. Holy Spirit + Scripture + Real Life. Avoid extremes.
- **中文**: 灵-道-生命平衡。圣灵+圣经+现实生活。避免极端。

**L2**: Tri-fold method. Function: Balance revelation, scripture, context. East: 天地人三才/道器合一。

### 4. Purging Dream (清理梦)

**L1**: **Processing/releasing** emotional or spiritual baggage. Like garbage disposal, working through issues. Common after intense experiences. May be disturbing but therapeutic function. Release not revelation. **处理/释放**情绪或灵性包袱。如垃圾处理，处理议题。强烈经历后常见。或令人不安但治疗功能。释放非启示。

**Key Term Analysis**:
| Term | Definition | Significance |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Purging Dream | Processing baggage | Cathartic |
| Release Function | Not revelation | Therapeutic |
| Emotional Baggage | Working through | Cleansing |

**Textual Criticism (Bilingual)**:
- **English**: Purging dream: processing/releasing baggage. Therapeutic, not prophetic. Release not revelation.
- **中文**: 清理梦:处理/释放包袱。治疗性,非先知性。释放非启示。

**L2**: Cathartic release. Function: Emotional/spiritual cleansing, not prophetic message. East: 清理梦/释放梦（类似梦林玄解清除业障梦）。

### 5. Garbage Dream (垃圾梦)

**L1**: **Meaningless**, result of daily stress, pizza before bed. Brain processing trivia. **Ignore these**, don't over-interpret. Not every dream has deep meaning. **无意义**，日常压力结果、睡前披萨。大脑处理琐事。**忽略这些**，勿过度诠释。非每个梦都有深意。

**Key Term Analysis**:
| Term | Definition | Significance |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Garbage Dream | Meaningless | Filter out |
| Daily Stress | Brain processing | Not spiritual |
| Ignore | Don't over-interpret | Practical advice |

**Textual Criticism (Bilingual)**:
- **English**: Garbage dream: meaningless, daily stress result. Ignore, don't over-interpret.
- **中文**: 垃圾梦:无意义,日常压力结果。忽略,勿过度诠释。

**L2**: Meaningless noise. Function: Filter out, don't interpret. East: 无意义梦/日常杂梦。

### 6. Internal Dream (内在梦)

**L1**: **Significant spiritual message** requiring interpretation. Categories: Personal Growth (character), Spiritual Warfare (demonic attack/intercession), Destiny/Calling (life purpose), Prophetic (future events/community). **Focus here** for interpretation. **重大灵性信息**需要诠释。类别：个人成长(品格)、灵性争战(魔鬼攻击/代祷)、命定/呼召(人生目的)、先知性(未来事件/社群)。**专注此类**诠释。

**Key Term Analysis**:
| Term | Definition | Significance |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Internal Dream | Significant message | Focus here |
| Four Categories | Growth/Warfare/Destiny/Prophetic | Classification |
| Spiritual Message | Needs interpretation | Divine guidance |

**Textual Criticism (Bilingual)**:
- **English**: Internal dream: significant spiritual message. Four categories. Focus for interpretation.
- **中文**: 内在梦:重大灵性信息。四类。诠释重点。

**L2**: Significant revelation. Function: Divine guidance in 4 areas (growth/warfare/destiny/prophetic). East: 有意义梦/灵性启示梦。

### 7. 4-Step Interpretation Method

**L1**: Toach's practical framework:  
(1) **Discern the Spirit** - from God, enemy, or flesh? Test spiritual source.  
(2) **Everything is Symbolic** - not literal, find spiritual meaning.  
(3) **Everything is About You** - personal revelation, not predicting others.  
(4) **Identify the Message** - what is God saying about your journey?  
Simple, applicable method vs academic approaches. Toach实用框架：(1)**辨别来源**-神、仇敌或肉体？测试灵性源头。(2)**一切是象征**-非字面找灵性含义。(3)**一切关于你**-个人启示非预测他人。(4)**识别信息**-神说关于你旅程什么？简单可应用方法vs学术方法。

**Key Term Analysis**:
| Term | Definition | Significance |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 4-Step Method | Practical framework | Simple, applicable |
| Discern Spirit | Source testing | God/enemy/flesh |
| About You | Personal revelation | Not predicting others |

**Textual Criticism (Bilingual)**:
- **English**: 4-step method: (1) discern source, (2) symbolic, (3) about you, (4) identify message.
- **中文**: 四步法:(1)辨别来源,(2)象征性,(3)关于你,(4)识别信息。

**L2**: Practical method. Function: 4 steps for spiritual dream interpretation. East: 四步解梦法（类似梦林玄解解梦次第）。

## PART 2: Representative Christian Symbols

### 8. Water (水) - Spiritual Meaning

**L1**: **Holy Spirit, cleansing, Word of God**. Clean water = Spirit's presence, purity. Dirty water = contaminated teaching, worldliness. Flood = overwhelming spiritual move. Drowning = overwhelmed by circumstances. Biblical: Water baptism, living water (John 4). **圣灵/洁净/神的话**。清水=灵的临在、纯洁。脏水=污染教导、世俗。洪水=压倒性灵性运动。溺水=被环境淹没。圣经：水洗礼、活水(约翰4章)。

**Key Term Analysis**:
| Term | Definition | Significance |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Water (Christian) | Holy Spirit/cleansing | Biblical meaning |
| Clean Water | Spirit's presence | Purity |
| Dirty Water | Contaminated teaching | Worldliness |

**Textual Criticism (Bilingual)**:
- **English**: Water: Holy Spirit, cleansing, Word. Clean=purity, Dirty=worldliness. Biblical: baptism, living water.
- **中文**: 水:圣灵,洁净,神的话。清=纯洁,脏=世俗。圣经:洗礼,活水。

**L2**: Holy Spirit/cleansing. Compare: Jung (emotions/unconscious), Toach (Spirit/Word). East: 水=情感(Jung共通) + 圣灵/道(Christian特有)。

**Key Term Analysis**:
| Term | Definition | Significance |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Flying (Christian) | Spiritual ascent | Heavenly realms |
| Intercessory Prayer | Soaring in Spirit | Ministry function |
| Third Heaven | 2 Cor 12:2 | Biblical reference |

**Textual Criticism (Bilingual)**:
- **English**: Flying: spiritual ascent, heavenly realms, prophetic vision. Intercessory prayer. Third heaven.
- **中文**: 飞行:灵性上升,天界,先知异象。代祷。三层天。

**L2**: Spiritual ascent. Compare: Jung (transcendence/freedom), Toach (prophetic vision/intercession). East: 飞升(共通) + 代祷/天界(Christian特有)。

Often enemy/deception in Christian interpretation. **仇敌/撒但**(创世记3蛇欺骗夏娃)、**智慧**(灵巧像蛇马太10:16)、或**医治**(铜蛇民数记21)。语境决定含义。Christian诠释常为仇敌/欺骗。

**Key Term Analysis**:
| Term | Definition | Significance |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Snake (Christian) | Enemy/wisdom/healing | Context-dependent |
| Satan/Serpent | Genesis 3 deception | Primary meaning |
| Bronze Serpent | Num 21 healing | Alternate meaning |

**Textual Criticism (Bilingual)**:
- **English**: Snake: enemy/Satan, wisdom, or healing. Context determines. Often enemy in Christian.
- **中文**: 蛇:仇敌/撒但,智慧,或医治。语境决定。Christian常为仇敌。

**L2**: Enemy/wisdom/healing. Compare: Jung (transformation/sexuality), Toach (spiritual warfare/biblical). East: 蛇=转化(Jung) vs 撒但/智慧(Christian)。

| Lion of Judah | Rev 5:5 Jesus | Kingship |
| Roaring Lion | 1 Pet 5:8 enemy | Prowling danger |

**Textual Criticism (Bilingual)**:

- **中文**: 狮子:耶稣(犹大狮子)或仇敌(吼叫狮子)。语境决定。勇敢,王权。
