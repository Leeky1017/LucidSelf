#### Source Text

"Car symbolizes ministry vehicle—the calling you're executing. Type reflects style: sports car = fast-paced ministry, truck = heavy-load. Driver = you're leading, passenger = you're following. Condition reflects ministry health."

#### English Paraphrase

**Car/Vehicle** = **ministry vehicle, calling execution**—how you're moving in your purpose (Christian-specific, not in Jung/Freud).

**Symbol Meanings**:
- **Type of Vehicle**: Ministry style (sports car = fast/dynamic, truck = heavy-load/endurance, bus = corporate/many people)
- **Driver Position**: Leading your calling, in control of ministry direction
- **Passenger**: Following another's lead, submitted to authority
- **Condition**: Ministry health (running well = healthy, broken = problems need fixing)
- **Direction**: Where ministry is heading (forward = progress, reverse = backsliding)

**Biblical Foundation**:
- Not explicitly biblical but practical application of calling metaphor
- Related to "running the race" (1 Cor 9:24, Heb 12:1)

**Comparison**:
- **Jung/Freud**: Don't address this symbol
- **Toach**: Ministry-specific interpretation

- **Driver vs Passenger**: Leading your calling vs following another's lead.

### 15. Clothing - Spiritual Covering

#### Key Term Analysis

<!-- L1_BEGIN -->
| Term | Definition | Significance |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Spiritual Covering | Anointing/protection | Core meaning |
| White Garments | Righteousness | Isa 61:10, Rev 7 |
| Prophetic Mantle | Anointing transfer | 2 Kgs 2:13 |
| Nakedness | Loss of covering | Vulnerability |

#### Source Text

"Clothing symbolizes spiritual covering and anointing. White = righteousness (Isaiah 61:10), Black = sin, Purple = royalty, Robe = prophetic mantle (2 Kings 2:13). Nakedness = loss of covering/shame."

#### English Paraphrase

**Clothing** = **spiritual covering, anointing, identity**—what covers and protects you spiritually.

**Symbol Meanings**:
- **White Garments**: Righteousness, purity, bridal garment (Isaiah 61:10, Rev 7:9-14)
- **Black Clothing**: Sin, mourning, darkness, need for cleansing
- **Purple**: Royalty, kingship, authority (Jesus mocked in purple, Mark 15:17)
- **Robe/Mantle**: Prophetic anointing (Elijah's mantle, 2 Kings 2:13)
- **Nakedness**: Loss of covering, shame, vulnerability, exposed (Gen 3:7)
- **New Clothes**: New season, new anointing, promotion

**Biblical Foundation**:
- Isaiah 61:10: "Clothed me with garments of salvation, robe of righteousness"
- Revelation 7:9: "Great multitude in white robes"
- 2 Kings 2:13: Elisha picked up Elijah's mantle (anointing transfer)

**Comparison**:
- **Jung**: Clothing = persona, social mask
- **Toach**: Clothing = spiritual covering (anointing not just social role)

#### Chinese Explanation

**衣服**=**灵性遮盖、膏抹、身份**——在灵性上遮盖和保护你的。

**符号含义**：白衣(公义、纯洁、新娘礼服-赛61:10,启7:9-14)；黑衣(罪、哀悼、黑暗、需洁净)；紫衣(王权、君王、权柄-耶稣被嘲穿紫袍,可15:17)；袍/外袍(先知膏抹-以利亚外袍,王下2:13)；赤裸(失遮盖、羞耻、脆弱、暴露-创3:7)；新衣(新季节、新膏抹、晋升)

**圣经基础**：赛61:10"给我穿上救恩衣、公义袍"；启7:9"许多人穿白衣"；王下2:13以利沙拾起以利亚外袍(膏抹转移)

**对比**：Jung(衣服=人格面具、社会面具)；Toach(衣服=灵性遮盖-膏抹非仅社会角色)

#### Core Points

- **Spiritual Covering**: Clothing = what covers and protects you spiritually (anointing, identity).
- **Colors**: White = righteousness; Black = sin/mourning; Purple = royalty.
- **Robe/Mantle**: Prophetic anointing (Elijah's mantle, 2 Kings 2:13).
- **Nakedness**: Loss of covering, shame, vulnerability (Genesis 3:7).
- **vs Jung**: Jung sees clothing as persona/social mask; Toach sees spiritual covering.

#### Detailed Explanation

Clothing symbolizes spiritual covering, anointing, and identity—what covers and protects you spiritually.
