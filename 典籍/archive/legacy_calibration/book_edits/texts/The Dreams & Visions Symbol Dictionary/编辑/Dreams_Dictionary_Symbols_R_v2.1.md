## 1. Rain 雨/祝福

### Source Text
> **Rain**: A blessing in a dry land—provision for all your needs, both physical and spiritual. Without rain, there is no harvest. Dreaming of rain falling down on you is a picture of the Lord sending His blessing.
> Negative: A rainstorm with hail or flooding speaks of curses and the enemy's attack.

| Storm | 风暴 | 仇敌的攻击 |
