### Core Thesis in One Sentence
> Astrology's symbolic language consists of three interlocking systems—planets (what part of the mind), signs (why/how it operates), and houses (where it manifests)—which must be read holistically like a living language rather than mechanically like a phrase book.

## L1 - Deep Comprehension Layer

### L1.1 Source Text and Context

**Source Text**:
> Signs, houses, and planets. Three distinct systems of symbols. Three vocabularies. Together they form astrology's holy trinity. Each serves a distinct purpose. Each answers a distinct set of questions. Without all three, astrology could not exist.
> In broad terms, signs are identity, while houses are the arena within which identity operates. Signs provide the psychological framework, the needs and fears, the attitudes and biases, with which we attack the houses. Houses indicate problems and issues. They represent tasks we must face.
> Signs symbolize processes that take place within the mind. [...] Houses are more concrete. They represent that which the mind observes.
> Planets are the third dimension of astrological symbolism. They represent the actual structure of the mind. Each one symbolizes a particular psychological function: intellect; emotions; self-imagery; the impulse toward intimacy.
> To put it all briefly, the three systems of symbols answer the questions what, how and why, and where. Always look first to the planet, which is the what. [...] Then use the sign to determine exactly what that planet wants and what methods it might best use to achieve those goals—the why and the how. Finally, look at the house. It answers the where.

### L3.2 Abstract Rule Extraction

**Rule ID**: `rule_inner_sky_triadic_interpretation`

**Rule Structure** (pseudo-code form):

```
WHEN interpreting(planet_P, sign_S, house_H):
  FIRST identify P.function    # what part of mind
  THEN determine S.motivation AND S.method    # why and how
  FINALLY locate H.arena    # where expressed
  RETURN synthesis(P.function, S.style, H.context)
WITH confidence = High
BECAUSE "the three systems of symbols answer the questions what, how and why, and where"
UNLESS context == horary OR context == mundane (different rules apply)
```

**Rule Operationalization**:

| Dimension | Explanation |

| Trigger operationalization | Given any planet-sign-house combination, apply this sequence |
| Conclusion operationalization | Generate interpretation statement: "[Planet] seeking to [sign motivation] through [sign method] primarily in the arena of [house domain]" |
