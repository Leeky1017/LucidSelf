## L1 - Deep Comprehension Layer

### L1.1 Source Text and Context

**Source Text**:
> The fundamental difficulty in astrological interpretation is that the birthchart generates too much information. When we look at the chart we see hundreds of statements, all of them valid, all of them vying for attention.
> GUIDELINE NUMBER ONE: Ignore everything until you have thoroughly grasped the sun, moon, and ascendant.
> Sun, moon, and ascendant stand head and shoulders above the other influences. They are the Primal Triad. Think of the primal triad as forming the skeleton of the character.
> Sun establishes identity. Perhaps the best word for the psychological function of the sun is ego.
> What about the moon? It represents the subjective elements in the personality: feelings, emotions, impressionability. We can call the moon the soul.
> The Ascendant is the wrapping placed over the interactions of the sun and moon. It is helpful to think of the ascendant as the mask. This mask is not a disguise but rather the personality's vehicle for expression.
> The archetypes can be mixed and matched in any way you please. We could say that he is the artist, with the soul of the genius, wearing the mask of the warrior.

### L3.2 Abstract Rule Extraction

**Rule ID**: `rule_inner_sky_chart_synthesis`

**Rule Structure** (pseudo-code form):

```
FUNCTION synthesize_birthchart(chart):
  # GUIDELINE 1: Establish Primal Triad foundation
  sun_bit = interpret_bit(Sun, Sun.sign, Sun.house)
  moon_bit = interpret_bit(Moon, Moon.sign, Moon.house)
  asc_interpretation = interpret_ascendant(Ascendant.sign)
  
  # Apply archetype formula for quick synthesis
  archetype_formula = format(
    "{Sun.sign.archetype}, with soul of {Moon.sign.archetype}, " +
    "wearing mask of {Ascendant.sign.archetype}"
  )
  
  primal_triad = synthesize(sun_bit, moon_bit, asc_interpretation)
  
  # GUIDELINE 2: Establish context through hemisphere emphasis
  hemisphere = determine_hemisphere_emphasis(chart.planet_positions)
  
  # GUIDELINE 3: Identify focalizing planets
  focalizers = find_dignified_or_prominent_planets(chart)
  
  # GUIDELINES 4-6: Integrate aspects, rulerships, fine details
  aspects = weave_in_aspects(chart)
  rulerships = analyze_house_rulers(chart)
  details = remaining_factors(chart)
  
  RETURN complete_synthesis(primal_triad, archetype_formula, hemisphere, 
                            focalizers, aspects, rulerships, details)

WITH confidence = High
BECAUSE "Sun, moon, and ascendant stand head and shoulders above the other influences"
UNLESS chart has dominant factors that overshadow the Primal Triad
```

**Rule Operationalization**:

| Dimension | Explanation |
|:---|:---|
| Trigger operationalization | For any birthchart requiring synthesis, apply the six guidelines in sequence; begin with Primal Triad before any other analysis |
| Conclusion operationalization | Produce layered interpretation: foundation (Primal Triad + archetype formula) then context (hemisphere) then accents (focalizers) then connections (aspects, rulerships) then details |
| Confidence rationale | High; this is Forrest's master synthesis method and has become the standard approach in psychological astrology education |
