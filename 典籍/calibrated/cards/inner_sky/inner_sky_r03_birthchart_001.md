| Concept | Literal Meaning | Deep Semantics | Modern Reconstruction |

### L3.2 Abstract Rule Extraction

**Rule ID**: `rule_inner_sky_chart_construction`

**Rule Structure** (pseudo-code form):

```
WHEN reading_birthchart(chart):
  FOR each planet P in chart:
    P.sign = celestial_position(P, birth_time)  # where in zodiac
    P.house = local_position(P, birth_time, birth_place)  # where relative to horizon
  signs_linked_to = "space itself" (absolute)
  houses_linked_to = "local horizon" (relative)
  interpretation = combine(P.sign, P.house) for all P
WITH confidence = High
BECAUSE "houses are linked to the local horizon, while signs are linked to space itself"
```

**Rule Operationalization**:

| Dimension | Explanation |
|:---|:---|
| Trigger operationalization | When examining any planet in a chart, distinguish its sign position (celestial) from house position (local) |
| Conclusion operationalization | Interpret sign as intrinsic quality, house as life context where that quality manifests |
| Confidence rationale | High; this is standard astrological chart construction |
