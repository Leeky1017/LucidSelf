### Core Thesis in One Sentence
> Aspects are geometric angles between planets that link separate "bits" into integrated patterns—conjunction fuses, opposition creates productive tension, square generates friction and growth pressure, trine provides harmony (with risk of laziness), and sextile produces excitation and mutual stimulation.

```
FUNCTION interpret_aspect(planet_A, planet_B, aspect_type):
  IF aspect_type == conjunction:
    RETURN "A and B are fused; one activates the other"
    quality = "neither good nor bad; depends on planets involved"
  IF aspect_type == opposition:
    RETURN "A and B are in tension; must hold both consciously"
    quality = "challenging but potentially balancing"
  IF aspect_type == square:
    RETURN "A and B in friction; neither can rest"
    quality = "challenging; promotes growth through pressure"
  IF aspect_type == trine:
    RETURN "A and B cooperate easily; may fall asleep"
    quality = "easy; risk of laziness; requires will to activate"
  IF aspect_type == sextile:
    RETURN "A and B excite each other; stimulating but unstable"
    quality = "dynamic; risk of giddiness"
    
WITH confidence = High
BECAUSE "aspects are the laws of grammar and syntax"
```
