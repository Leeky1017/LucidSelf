### Core Thesis in One Sentence
> Planets are the active agents in astrology—psychological functions that "ignite the system" by channeling zodiacal energies into specific compartments of consciousness (ego, emotion, intellect, relationship, will, etc.), each with healthy expression (Function) and unhealthy expression (Dysfunction).

### L3.2 Abstract Rule Extraction

**Rule ID**: `rule_inner_sky_planet_interpretation`

**Rule Structure** (pseudo-code form):

```
FOR each planet P:
  P.function = healthy_psychological_expression(P)
  P.dysfunction = distorted_expression(P)
  P.key_questions = developmental_themes(P)
  IF P.retrograde:
    P.expression = introverted_version(P.function)
    P.note = "independent but potentially withdrawn"

WHEN interpreting planet_placement(P in sign S in house H):
  RETURN P.function colored by S.quality expressed in H.arena
  WITH dysfunction_risk = P.dysfunction colored by S.shadow
WITH confidence = High
BECAUSE "Each planet symbolizes a certain compartment in human consciousness"
```
