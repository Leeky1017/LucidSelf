### Core Thesis in One Sentence
> The sky itself is astrology's foundational symbol representing the Absolute/undifferentiated consciousness; through the seasonal cycle (elements) and life cycle phases (modes), this prime symbol is broken down into the twelve signs—each representing both a personality type and an evolutionary method.
