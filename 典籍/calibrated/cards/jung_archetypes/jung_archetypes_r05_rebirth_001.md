### Core Thesis in One Sentence
> Rebirth is a universal archetypal motif manifesting in five distinct forms—metempsychosis, reincarnation, resurrection, participation mystique, and transformation—with the psychological reality lying in subjective experience of renewal rather than metaphysical claims.
