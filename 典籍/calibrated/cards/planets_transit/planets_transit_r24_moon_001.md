> The transiting Moon, while generally fleeting in its effects (hours to days), reveals unconscious emotional patterns and habitual reactions, with its influence amplified significantly during lunations (new/full Moon) and eclipses on natal planets.

## L1 - Deep Comprehension Layer

### L1.1 Source Text and Context

**Source Text**:
> "The significance of the transiting Moon is very transient, because the Moon moves so fast. Even though the natal Moon is one of the most powerful points in your horoscope, its transits may go by without too much notice. When it is transiting natal planets its effects are felt for a couple of hours at the most. Transits of the Moon through the houses last a couple of days."
>
> "Exceptions to this rule occur whenever there is a major change of phase — new Moon, full Moon, etc. — upon a natal planet. Under these circumstances, you may feel the influence for one to four weeks. The effect of an eclipse on a natal planet may be felt up to several months."
>
> "But by itself the transiting Moon is felt mostly as changes of mood, transient encounters with others, and passing feelings. Often you may not even be conscious of its effects, because they may occur unconsciously."
