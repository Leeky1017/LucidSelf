### Core Thesis in One Sentence
> Venus operates as a Yin (receptive) planet that draws experiences and people to the native rather than actively pursuing them, manifesting as love, creativity, and social pleasure when balanced with Mars, but potentially as passive self-indulgence or manipulation when distorted.

> "Only a human being who has become as perfect a manifestation as possible of what he or she is can experience love. The balance between Mars and Venus is rather delicate and easily upset."
