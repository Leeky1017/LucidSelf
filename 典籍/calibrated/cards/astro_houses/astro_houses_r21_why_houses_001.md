|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| `locality-centered` | Astrology based on local horizon and visible sky | Tribal consciousness bound to specific geography, where cosmos mirrors local community | Pre-individual collective consciousness where identity is inseparable from place |
| `globe-centered` | Earth as rotating sphere in heliocentric system | Consciousness of human as member of planetary humanity, able to abstract from locality | Modern scientific worldview enabling universal rather than tribal perspective |
| `person-centered` | Individual as center of astrological interpretation | The person is the only truly fixed frame of reference; horizon and zenith move with the individual | Psychological astrology where birth chart is a mandala for self-actualization |
| `eight watches` | Original eight-fold division of day/night cycle | Vitalistic rhythm of solar cycle divided for practical agricultural/social purposes | Foundation for later twelve-house system, based on Sun's apparent motion |
